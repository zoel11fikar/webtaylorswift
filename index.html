Taylor Alison Swift (born December 13, 1989) is an American singer, songwriter, and actress.

She debuted in the country music industry but later transitioned to the pop genre. Swift is known for her songs that narrate personal experiences and daily life, as well as her storytelling ability through beautiful lyrics.
